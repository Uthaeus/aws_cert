Data Warehousing


-Used for business intelligence
    -Tools like:
        -Cognos
        -JasperSoft
        -SQL Server
        -Reporting Services
        -Oracle Hyperion
        -SAP NetWeaver
-Used to pull in very large and complex data sets
-Usually used by management to do queries on data 
    -such as current performance vs targets, etc.

OLTP vs OLAP
-Online Transaction Processing differs from Online Analytics Processing in terms of the types of queries you will run

-Data Warehousing databases use different type of architecture both from a database perspective and infrastructure layer
