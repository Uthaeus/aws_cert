Cross Account Access 


-Makes it easier for you to work productively within a multi-account (or multi-role) AWS environment by making it easy for you to switch roles 

->Steps:
    -Identify account numbers 
    -Create a group in IAM
    -Create a user in IAM
    -Log in to Production
    -Create the bucket policy
    -Create the Cross Account Role 
    -Apply the newly created policy to the role 
    -Log in to the new account 
    -Create a new inline policy 
    -Apply it to the new group 
    -Log in as new user 
    -Switch accounts
