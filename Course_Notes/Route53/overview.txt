Route53


Routing Policies:
    -Simple
    -Weighted
        -directs amount of traffic
    -Latency
        -directs based on lowest latency 
    -Failover
        -directs based on health of endpoint
    -Geolocation
        -directs based on geographical location

-Route53 allows up to 50 domain names
    -this is a soft limit which can be raised by contacting AWS support
